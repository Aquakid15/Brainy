# Brainy

An addictive Flappy Bird-inspired arcade game for iOS that tests your reflexes and timing.

## About

Brainy is a challenging tap-to-play game where you navigate through obstacles by timing your taps perfectly. The simple mechanics make it easy to learn, but the increasing difficulty keeps you coming back for "just one more try."

## Features

- **Simple Controls**: One-tap gameplay - tap to fly!
- **Challenging**: Easy to learn, hard to master
- **High Scores**: Track your personal best and compete against yourself
- **Clean Design**: Beautiful, distraction-free interface
- **Offline Play**: Play anytime, anywhere without internet
- **Free to Play**: Available now on the iOS App Store

## How to Play

1. Tap anywhere on the screen to make your character fly upward
2. Navigate through gaps between obstacles
3. Avoid hitting obstacles or it's game over
4. Challenge yourself to beat your high score!
